AverageDirectionalIndex (ADX)

PART 1: MAIN GUIDE

1. Identity

Full Name: Average Directional Index

Abbreviation: ADX

Category: Trend Strength

Creator: J. Welles Wilder Jr. (1978)

One-Sentence Definition: ADX is the definitive trend-strength indicator that tells you *how strong* a trend is (but not which direction)—it's the market's "speedometer" that separates trending from ranging environments.

2. Calculation Logic

Formula

$$+DI = \frac{Smoothed(+DM)}{Smoothed(TR)} \times 100$$

$$-DI = \frac{Smoothed(-DM)}{Smoothed(TR)} \times 100$$

$$DX = \frac{|+DI - (-DI)|}{+DI + (-DI)} \times 100$$

$$ADX = Wilder\ Smoothing(DX, n)$$

StepFormulaPurpose
1$+DM = H_t - H_{t-1}$ (if > -DM and > 0)Positive directional movement
2$-DM = L_{t-1} - L_t$ (if > +DM and > 0)Negative directional movement
3$TR = \max(H-L,H-C_{prev},L-C_{prev})$True Range
4Smooth DM and TR with Wilder method14-period smoothing
5Calculate +DI and -DIDirectional indicators
6Calculate DX from DI differenceRaw directional index
7Smooth DX to get ADXFinal trend strength

Real-World Analogy

ADX is a speedometer for the market. A reading of 15 means the market is barely moving (no trend). A reading of 50 means the market is racing (strong trend). Like a speedometer, it doesn't tell you *which direction* you're going—just how fast. Use +DI/-DI for direction.

False Signal Scenarios

1. Sub-25 DI Crosses

DI crossovers when ADX < 20 are noise, not signals.

2. High ADX Trap

ADX at 60 doesn't mean "buy more"—it often precedes exhaustion.

3. Direction Confusion

Rising ADX means "trend strengthening" but could be bearish if -DI > +DI.

8. Pro Insights

The ADX Pillow Setup

After a long downtrend, if price flattens and ADX drops below 15 (extreme dormancy), the ADX acts like a "pillow" for price. When price suddenly breaks out and ADX turns up from this sub-15 level, expect a massive multi-month trend. The longer the compression, the bigger the eventual move.

The Hook Exit

When ADX rises above 40-50 and then hooks down (slope turns negative), immediately tighten stops or exit. The trend's acceleration phase is over. This "Hook" signal often appears 5-10 bars before price shows any weakness.

ADX as a Position Sizing Tool

Many professionals use ADX for dynamic position sizing:

- ADX 25-30: Normal position

- ADX 30-40: Increased position (trend confirmed)

- ADX 40-50: Reduce position (late to the party)

- ADX > 50: Minimum position (trend exhausting)

9. Summary

"ADX is the gatekeeper that answers the most important question in trading: Should I be trend-following or mean-reverting right now? Above 25, follow trends. Below 20, fade extremes."

PART 2: SUPPLEMENTARY GUIDE

1. Core Concept

ADX doesn't care about direction—only intensity. Think of it as measuring the "decisiveness" of the market. A high ADX means buyers OR sellers are clearly winning. A low ADX means nobody's in control.

The Three Components:

1. +DI (Green line): Buying pressure strength

2. -DI (Red line): Selling pressure strength

3. ADX (Black line): How much one side dominates

Signal Patterns

ADX Trend Entry Signal

The ideal ADX entry combines four conditions: ADX above 25 (confirming a trend exists), rising ADX (trend strengthening), +DI above -DI (bullish direction), and a fresh +DI/-DI crossover. This combination captures the moment when a confirmed trend gains fresh directional momentum, providing the highest probability entries.

ADX Hook Exit Signal

When ADX rises above 50 (extreme trend strength) and then begins declining, this "Hook" pattern warns of trend exhaustion. The trend has peaked in intensity and will likely slow or reverse. Professional traders use this signal to exit positions 5-10 bars before price shows visible weakness.


def adx_trend_entry(adx, plus_di, minus_di):
    """Primary ADX trend-following entry"""
    trend_exists = adx[-1] > 25
    trend_rising = adx[-1] > adx[-2]
    bullish = plus_di[-1] > minus_di[-1]
    di_cross = plus_di[-2] <= minus_di[-2] and plus_di[-1] > minus_di[-1]
    
    return trend_exists and trend_rising and bullish and di_cross

def adx_hook_exit(adx):
    """ADX Hook exhaustion signal"""
    was_extreme = any(x > 50 for x in adx[-5:])
    turning_down = adx[-1] < adx[-2]
    
    return was_extreme and turning_down
