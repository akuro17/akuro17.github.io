Elder Ray

PART 1: MAIN GUIDE

1. Identity

Full Name: Elder Ray

Abbreviation: ER

Category: Momentum / Power Balance

Creator: Dr. Alexander Elder

One-Sentence Definition: Elder Ray measures Bull Power (deviation of highs from EMA) and Bear Power (deviation of lows from EMA) to quantify the strength of buyers and sellers relative to the consensus value.

2. Calculation Logic

Formula

$$\text{Bull Power} = High - EMA_{13}(Close)$$

$$\text{Bear Power} = Low - EMA_{13}(Close)$$

ComponentFormulaInterpretation
Bull Power$High - EMA$Positive = Bulls pushing above value
Bear Power$Low - EMA$Negative = Bears pushing below value
EMA13-periodRepresents consensus value

Real-World Analogy

Think of Elder Ray like tug-of-war meters. The EMA is the "center" where the rope should be. Bull Power measures how far bulls pulled the rope toward their side (highs). Bear Power measures how far bears pulled toward their side (lows). In a healthy uptrend, bulls consistently dominate.

Primary Buy Signal (Elder's Method)


IF EMA(13) > EMA(13)[1]           // Trend is UP
AND BearPower < 0                  // Current dip below value
AND BearPower > BearPower[1]       // Bears losing strength
THEN → BUY (Pullback in uptrend with weakening selling)

7. Practical Cautions

Scale Dependency: Bull/Bear Power are in price units. A Bull Power of 5 means different things on a $10 stock vs. Bitcoin at $60,000. Do not use absolute thresholds.
First 12 Bars: EMA is null, therefore Bull/Bear Power is null.

8. Pro Insights

The Kangaroo Tail: A massive spike in Bear Power (very long bar downward) that immediately snaps back indicates panic selling absorbed by strong buyers. If this occurs far below the EMA, it's a powerful reversal signal.

Divergence is King: Don't focus on absolute histogram height. Focus on the slope difference between price lows and Bear Power lows. Divergence = exhaustion.

Use in Uptrends Only: Elder designed Bear Power for buying opportunities in uptrends. Look for small negative bars (minor dips) recovering—not for shorting.

9. Summary

"Elder Ray is your market power meter—Bull Power shows how hard bulls are pushing above value, Bear Power shows bears pushing below. Trade with the stronger side."

PART 2: SUPPLEMENTARY GUIDE

1. Core Concept

Elder Ray answers: "Who is stronger right now—bulls or bears?"

The 13-EMA represents the "fair value" or consensus price. When High exceeds EMA, bulls have pushed price above value. When Low is below EMA, bears have pushed below. The relative extremity and direction of these deviations reveal market power.

2. Statistical Edge

Elder Ray pullback entries in confirmed uptrends show ~60% win rate with favorable reward-to-risk when stops are placed below the recent swing low.

3. Actionable Strategy: "Bear Power Pullback"


SETUP: EMA(13) rising for 5+ bars (confirmed uptrend)
SIGNAL: Bear Power < 0 (price dipped below EMA)
        AND Bear Power > Bear Power[1] (bears weakening)
ENTRY: Buy at close
STOP: Recent swing low or EMA - 1×ATR
TARGET: Trail with EMA until Bear Power makes new low

4. Risks & Limitations

- Scale issues: Cannot compare across assets

- Flash crash distortion: Extreme moves cause extreme readings

- Relative, not absolute: Use slopes, not levels

PART 3: ADVANCED SIGNAL REFERENCE

Signal Patterns

Elder Ray Buy Signal

This classic pullback entry signal triggers when three conditions align: uptrend (EMA rising), price dip below value (Bear Power negative), and weakening selling pressure (Bear Power rising from lows). Dr. Elder's method identifies ideal buying opportunities where bears are losing strength within an established bull trend, offering favorable risk-reward entries.


def elder_ray_buy(ema, ema_prev, bear_power, bear_power_prev):
    uptrend = ema > ema_prev
    dip = bear_power < 0
    bears_weakening = bear_power > bear_power_prev
    
    return uptrend and dip and bears_weakening

Screening Filters


SET EMA_13 = EMA(Close, 13)
SET BearPower = Low - EMA_13

FILTER =
  (EMA_13 > EMA_13[1]) AND          // Uptrend
  (BearPower < 0) AND                // Currently dipping
  (BearPower > BearPower[1])         // Bears weakening

Divergence Detection


IF Price makes Lower_Low
AND BearPower makes Higher_Low
THEN BULLISH_DIVERGENCE = True
